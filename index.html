Ruler of the Plane is a collection of medieval-styled games that are based on geometric concepts.

There are four games in the original collection: conquer (Voronoi diagrams), divide (ham-sandwich theorem), illuminate (art gallery problem) and connect (MSTs, spanners and TSP). There are also branches like for dots and polygons (a geometric variant of dots and boxes).

The game has been implemented using Unity and is freely available to play online or to download.
